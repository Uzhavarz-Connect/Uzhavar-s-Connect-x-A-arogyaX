Agriculture Robotics
🤖 SLAMbots: Autonomous Agricultural Fleet 🚜

We developed a simulation for a fleet of SLAM-enabled agricultural robots (Slambots) to enhance farm supervision through distributed monitoring and autonomous navigation.

🌐 Simulation & Web App Focus

We simulated fleet behavior in Webots 🖥️ with sensor-based data generation.

Our focus was to develop a web app for a fleet of Slambots that can:

  • Navigate: Use SLAM for large farmlands 🗺️.
  • Distributed Monitoring: Collect & analyze soil moisture 💧, temperature 🌡️, and crop health 🌱.
  • Adaptive Collaboration: Share data for optimized task distribution 🤝.
  • Fail-Safe Operations: Handle sensor failures 🚫, communication delays ⏳, and power constraints 🔋.
  • Remote Supervision: Enable farmer monitoring via a web/mobile interface 📱.

Constraints Handled 🚧

  • Limited Communication: Predictive decision-making required 📡.
  • Low-Power Optimization: Minimize energy consumption ⚡.
  • Sensor Failures & Redundancy: Handle dropouts/inaccuracies 📉.
  • Localization Without GPS: Alternative mapping techniques 📍.
  • Multi-Robot Coordination: Optimize fleet-wide task execution 🔄.

Integrated Agricultural System
Smart Agricultural Monitoring & Crop Management System using IoT, Computer Vision, YOLO, Deep Learning, Robotics, NLP, Flask, React & Capacitor (Video Demo) (with Solidworks simulation software):

• Developed an integrated solution using IoT sensors, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning (YOLO) for real-time crop disease detection, weed identification, and pest monitoring in TN Specific Crops such as Sugarcane, Mango, Coconut & Paddy.

• Engineered a multi-terrain robotic system combining land rover, aerial drones for pesticide spraying, and an amphibious hydro vehicle fitted with custom-trained ML models & Sensors for comprehensive field monitoring in Dense, Aquatic & Sparse vegetations.

• Implemented automated alarming systems that notify farmers of detected issues through SMS and mobile app notifications, handled through Twilio API.

• Created a Multilingual Multimodal conversational AI Chatbot using ElevanLabs & Vapi to assist farmers with crop management decisions, which is accessible in regional languages to improve agricultural knowledge sharing.

• Designed a user-friendly dashboard displaying analytical insights, predictive crop health metrics, and recommended interventions.

Healthcare Management
A'arogyaX: AI-Powered Resilient & Adaptive Healthcare Management System

Developed AI agent based doctor appointment booking & scheduling system with priority scheduling algorithms for emergency cases and QR-based/biometric prepaid payment systems for efficient and faster receipt generation to avoid queues, aiding with financial assistance options such as loan/installment, collateral guarantees for rural patients who can't afford the fee of a surgery in that moment.

Implemented blockchain based disaster-resilient supply chain analyzing some of the shortest path graph traversal algorithms, ranging from BFS, DFS, PRIMS, KRUSKAL, MST, RRT, RRT*, DIJKSTRA, A* - all compared & plotted on a 3D cross section Map using gmaps & places API, with real time satellite view using zoom earth and GEE, with digital twin simulation for contingency planning and alternative routing during disruptions, monitoring end-to-end supply chain from production to delivery.

Created Doctor's PA chatbot as a conversational multimodal multilingual assistant integrating OCR models such as small docling & mistral large 7b for understanding Doctor's Handwriting and leveraging existing medical LLMs such as Google's MedPalm, MedLM, BcpMLP, PubMed and Microsoft's Dragon Copilot for schedule management, automated non workflows with Google Calendar API for checking appointment schedules, and inventory management to check medicine stock availability for prescriptions.

Sign Language Translation
LINGUA: Sign Language Converter for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing Community

Developed a comprehensive Multimodal solution that translates Indian Sign Language (ISL) into Multilingual text, speech (regional languages) integrating Sarvam AI & gTTS, images, and video in real-time using landmark pose estimation and motion recognition techniques.

Implemented real-time voice cloning with lip synced Avatar Clones that handle Hierarchical prosody modeling in Speech Synthesis and Morse code integration to enhance communication options for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals.

Created educational gesture-based games using Computer Vision to teach sign language fundamentals to younger users, increasing accessibility and engagement from a younger age understanding the importance of gestures, so that they need not feel alienated from the society.

Engineered specialized hardware including sensor-equipped gloves for the elderly and paralyzed, that is capable of detecting finger bend movements and mapping them to common phrases like "restroom," "food," and "sleep", notifying the guardian.

Designed assistive technologies including eye-gaze controlled keyboards and gesture-based menu ordering systems to support elderly and paralyzed individuals.

Developed domain-specific sign language representations for railway announcements, healthcare, fintech, agriculture, everyday educational terms, sports, food sector, rhymes & storytelling for children and legal terminology using 3D blender avatars and stick figure animations.
